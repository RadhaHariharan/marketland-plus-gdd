🌟 13 — Farm Mastery

Farm Mastery is the star-rating system for each individual crop and animal on the farm. As players grow more of a specific crop or collect more from a specific animal, they earn Mastery Stars that permanently improve yield, speed, and value for that item.

⭐ How Mastery Works

Each crop and animal has its own Mastery Level from 0 to 3 stars, tracked separately:
- Mastery progress is measured in total harvests (crops) or total collections (animals)
- Stars are permanent — they do not reset
- Stars provide compounding bonuses as they accumulate

🌾 Crop Mastery Thresholds

Star Level Harvests Required Bonus
⭐ 1 Star 25 harvests +10% yield per plot
⭐⭐ 2 Stars 100 harvests +20% yield, +5% Farm Token bonus
⭐⭐⭐ 3 Stars 300 harvests +35% yield, +10% FT, wilt timer +50%

Example for Wheat:
- Base yield: 8–12 per harvest
- At 3 Stars: 10.8–16.2 per harvest (+35%)
- Wilt timer: 45 min instead of 30 min (+50%)

🐄 Animal Mastery Thresholds

Star Level Collections Required Bonus
⭐ 1 Star 50 collections +10% product quality
⭐⭐ 2 Stars 200 collections +20% quality, chance of bonus product doubles
⭐⭐⭐ 3 Stars 500 collections +35% quality, +25% chance of special drop, happiness regenerates 20% faster

🏆 Building Mastery

Processing buildings also have mastery (see 09-processing-buildings.md for details):

Star Level Jobs Required Bonus
⭐ 1 Star 50 jobs +5% yield
⭐⭐ 2 Stars 200 jobs +10% yield, +1 queue slot
⭐⭐⭐ 3 Stars 500 jobs +20% yield, −15% time, exclusive recipe unlocked

🎯 Farm Mastery Score

A player's Total Farm Mastery Score is the sum of all individual mastery stars across crops, animals, and buildings. This score is visible on the farm overview screen and in the social leaderboard.

Score Range Farm Rank
0–10 stars Apprentice Farmer
11–25 stars Skilled Farmer
26–50 stars Expert Farmer
51–75 stars Master Farmer
76+ stars Legendary Farmer

📊 Mastery UI

The Farm Mastery screen shows:
- Grid of all crops/animals/buildings with star status
- Progress bar to next star for selected item
- Total Farm Mastery Score
- Rank badge
- "Highest Mastery" crops/animals highlighted
