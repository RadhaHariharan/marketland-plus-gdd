# 🐄 08 — Animal Husbandry

Animals are the second pillar of the farm, producing ingredients that cannot be grown in crop plots. Their products are central to mid-to-late-game processing chains and unlock some of the highest-value store items.

## 🍽️ Feeding System

Animals must be fed to produce products. Feed is sourced from the farm itself:

| Feed Type | Source | Required By |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Grain (Wheat) | Wheat harvest | Chickens, Ducks |
| Hay | Wheat → Barn process | Cows, Sheep, Goats |
| Corn Feed | Corn harvest | Pigs |
| Corn + Carrot mix | Both crops | Deer |
| Lavender / Sunflower | Crop plots | Bees (auto-grazed) |
| Carrot | Carrot harvest | Rabbits |

### Feeding States:
- **Well Fed** (green): Full production rate
- **Hungry** (yellow): 50% production rate; yellow indicator over pen
- **Starving** (red): Production stopped; animal happiness drops
- **Animal Keeper** worker: Automatically feeds all animals on schedule

## 😊 Animal Happiness System

Each animal has a **Happiness Score** (0–100):

| Happiness | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| 80–100 (Happy) | +25% product quality, chance of bonus product |
| 50–79 (Content) | Normal production |
| 20–49 (Sad) | −20% yield |
| 0–19 (Unhappy) | Production paused |

### Raising Happiness:
- Feed on schedule (+20 happiness)
- Clean pen (+10 happiness) — tap to clean, or Animal Keeper does it
- Upgrade pen (+5 permanent happiness)
- Pet the animal (tap interaction, once per hour) (+5 happiness)
- Rainbow weather (+10 happiness all animals)
