# 🗺️ 14 — Farm Expansion

Farm expansion is the long-term strategic layer of the farm system. Players unlock new zones as they level up, each adding more land, new building slots, and new crop/animal opportunities.

## 🏗️ The Greenhouse

The Greenhouse is a special enclosed zone that:
- Ignores all seasonal effects (crops grow at base speed year-round)
- Required for: Vanilla (Lv.35), Saffron (Lv.50), Truffle (Lv.60), Exotic Orchid (Lv.70)
- Has its own separate grid (12×8 tiles, expandable)
- Costs extra to maintain: **200 FT/week** upkeep (can be waived by Botanist)
- Visually distinct: glass building with interior lighting

### Greenhouse Expansion Slots:
| Slot Tier | Unlock | Extra Tiles | Cost |
|-----------|--------|------------|------|
| Basic | Lv.40 | 12×8 | Included |
| Extended | Lv.55 | +6×8 | 15,000 coins + 300 FT |
| Premium | Lv.72 | +8×8 | 40,000 coins + 600 FT |

## 🐝 The Apiary Zone

A dedicated area for beehives:
- Bees auto-graze on nearby Lavender and Sunflower plots
- Honey quality scales with proximity to bee-food crops
- Lavender within 5 tiles: +10% honey quality
- Sunflower within 5 tiles: +5% honey quality, +10% beeswax
- Multiple hive clusters can be placed in the apiary

---

## 🦌 The Deer Range

The largest single animal zone:
- Requires 4×4 per deer
- Natural terrain features (trees, water feature) boost Deer Happiness
- Rare Leather and Velvet products go directly to Luxury Fashion tier
- Only accessible with Zone 10 unlocked at Lv.80

---

## 💰 Zone Expansion Costs

Zones can be unlocked either by:
1. **Leveling up** and paying coins/FT (standard)
2. **Instant unlock** with Cash (costs 10–30 Cash depending on zone)

> 💡 **Design Note:** Players are never required to expand quickly. All farm zones are bonus content — the base Zones 0–3 provide ample space for casual play through Level 30+.

---

## 🗺️ Farm Layout Customisation

Within each zone, players freely arrange:
- Crop plots (any size: 1×1 to 4×4)
- Animal pens (fixed size per species)
- Processing buildings (2×2 to 4×4)
- Decorative elements (fences, trees, paths)
- Worker paths (auto-generated based on building placement)

### Layout Score
The game calculates a **Layout Efficiency Score** based on:
- Worker path length between buildings
- Distance from barn to processing buildings
- Distance from processing to supply lane
- % of available tiles used

Higher efficiency = more goods processed per hour with the same number of workers.
