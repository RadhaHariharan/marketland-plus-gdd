# 🌸 12 — Seasons & Weather

The living world of Marketland+ changes with seasons and weather, creating a dynamic environment that rewards planning and adds surprise to every play session. Seasons rotate every 4 real-world days; weather events occur randomly within each season.

---

## 🗓️ The Four Seasons

Each season lasts **4 real-world days** and cycles continuously: Spring → Summer → Autumn → Winter → Spring.

### 🌸 Spring
**Duration:** 4 days | **Visuals:** Blooming flowers, green fields, cherry blossoms

| Effect | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| All crops | +20% faster growth |
| Lavender yield | +50% |
| Health & Beauty store sales | +15% |
| Spring Event: **Bloom Festival** | Bonus quests, floral decorations available |

**Best Crops:** Lavender, Carrot, Strawberry
**Strategy:** Maximise lavender plots; launch Health & Beauty campaign

---

### ☀️ Summer
**Duration:** 4 days | **Visuals:** Bright sun, dry grass, vibrant colours

| Effect | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| Fruit crops (Tomato, Corn, Cucumber, Sunflower) | +25% yield |
| Wilt speed | 25% faster — watch your crops! |
| Cold drinks store sales | +30% |
| Summer Event: **Beach Blast** | Tropical decorations, exclusive customer (Tourist) |

**Best Crops:** Tomato, Corn, Cucumber, Sunflower, Sugarcane, Chilli
**Strategy:** Harvest frequently; stock cold beverages; consider extra Field Hand

---

### 🍂 Autumn
**Duration:** 4 days | **Visuals:** Orange leaves, golden fields, harvest colours

| Effect | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| All harvests | +10% coin value |
| Food displays | +10% customer spend |
| Autumn Event: **Harvest Festival** | Special campaign bonus, pumpkin decorations |
| Grapes, Hops yield | +20% (seasonal peak) |

**Best Crops:** Wheat, Potato, Grapes, Hops
**Strategy:** Save Harvest Cards for this season; run Harvest Mega Event campaign

---

### ❄️ Winter
**Duration:** 4 days | **Visuals:** Snow, frost, twinkling lights

| Effect | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| Outdoor crop growth | 50% slower |
| Greenhouse crops | Unaffected |
| Hot drinks store sales | +25% |
| Winter Event: **Winter Wonderland** | Holiday decorations, Gift Shopper customer |

**Best Crops:** Greenhouse crops (Vanilla, Saffron, Truffle, Orchid)
**Strategy:** Switch farm focus to greenhouse; stock hot beverages; decorate for LP bonus

---

## ⛈️ The Five Weather Events

Weather events occur randomly within any season and last **2–12 hours**. Each event has unique effects on both the farm and the store.

---

### 🌧️ Rain
**Duration:** 2–6 hours | **Frequency:** Common

| Effect | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| Auto-waters all crop plots | No watering needed |
| All crop yields | +10% |
| Customer foot traffic | +15% more customers |
| Mood | Cheerful; customers spend slightly more |

**Strategy:** Great time to plant large plots you wouldn't normally have time to water manually.

---

### ⛈️ Thunderstorm
**Duration:** 1–4 hours | **Frequency:** Uncommon

| Effect | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| Random crop plots | 1–3 plots struck by lightning (red alert) |
| Struck plots | Yield reduced to 0 unless player taps to save within 10 minutes |
| Customer foot traffic | −25% |
| Supplier shipments | Delayed by 30 minutes |

**Strategy:** Stay alert for lightning alerts; consider hiring a Field Hand to auto-handle emergencies.

---

### 🌞 Heatwave
**Duration:** 3–8 hours | **Frequency:** Summer-heavy

| Effect | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| Wilt speed | 3× normal — urgent harvesting required! |
| Cold beverages store sales | +50% |
| Ice cream / Juice displays | +60% customer spend |
| Animal water consumption | +50% (Animal Keeper needed) |

**Strategy:** Harvest all ready crops immediately; launch a cold drinks display campaign.

---

### 🌫️ Fog
**Duration:** 2–6 hours | **Frequency:** Uncommon

| Effect | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| Animal production rate | −20% |
| Mystery Shopper | 3× chance of appearing |
| Store visibility | Foggy visual effect (cosmetic) |

**Strategy:** Accept lower farm production; the Mystery Shopper bonus often more than compensates.

---

### 🌈 Rainbow
**Duration:** 30 minutes | **Frequency:** Rare (most valuable!)

| Effect | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| Next harvest Farm Token yield | 2× |
| Luxury customer spawns | +20% |
| All animal happiness | +10 |
| Rare collection item drop | 5% chance |

**Strategy:** Harvest everything immediately when Rainbow appears — this is the highest Farm Token event in the game.
