🎨 20 — Decorations

🧮 LP Calculation

Total LP = Sum of LP from all placed items + Floor LP + Wall LP + Upgrade bonuses
- LP is permanent — removing an item reduces LP
- LP auras from Attractions multiply nearby decoration LP by up to 1.25×
