# 🎪 19 — Attractions

Attractions are large, high-LP installations that significantly increase customer foot traffic and customer type quality. They don't sell products directly but create an environment that draws more customers and higher-spending visitors.

## ⚙️ Attraction Mechanics

### Passive Effects
All attractions provide a **continuous aura effect** to their surrounding tiles:
- Radius: 5 tiles
- All displays within radius receive +10% customer visit frequency
- Stacking: Multiple attractions stack (diminishing returns after 3)

### Active Events (Some Attractions)
Certain attractions have **active event modes** triggered by the player:
- 🎹 Grand Piano: "Live Concert" event (costs 5 Shopper Cards) — 1 hour of +50% all spend
- 🎭 Stage: "Performance Night" (costs 10 Shopper Cards) — 2 hours of +40% all customers
- 🍳 Live Kitchen Demo: "Chef's Special" (uses 1 farm ingredient) — 3 hours of +30% food sales

### LP Aura
Some attractions project an **LP aura** that adds bonus LP to nearby decorations:
- Fountain: +20% LP to decorations within 4 tiles
- Grand Piano: +15% LP to displays within 3 tiles
- VIP Lounge: +25% LP to luxury items within 5 tiles

## 💡 Placement Tips

> 💡 **Design Note:** Place attractions near the centre of the store rather than corners. A central attraction acts as a "magnet," pulling customers past more displays before they reach it and on the way out.

| Strategy | Placement |
|----------|-----------|
| Maximise foot traffic | Centre of store |
| Boost luxury customers | Near luxury displays (Zone 6/7) |
| Boost family customers | Near baby/toy section |
| Boost Fresh interest | Farm Corner near food section |
