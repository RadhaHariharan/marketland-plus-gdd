# 🎨 20 — Decorations

Decorations are the primary source of Luxury Points (LP) in the store. They have no direct revenue effect, but by raising LP they unlock wealthier customer types who spend dramatically more per visit.

## 🧮 LP Calculation

Total LP = Sum of LP from all placed items + Floor LP + Wall LP + Upgrade bonuses
- LP is **permanent** — removing an item reduces LP
- LP auras from Attractions multiply nearby decoration LP by up to 1.25×
