🏗️ 21 — Store Expansion

Store expansion is the primary long-term strategic investment in the store half of the game. Each new zone unlocks more floor space, new display categories, and higher-tier attractions.

🗺️ Zone Expansion Table

📐 Zone Layout Recommendations

Zone 1-2: The Essential Market

  • Food & Grocery displays (rows 1–3)
  • Beverages (row 4)
  • Basic coin-op items (ATM, vending)
  • Small decorations and plants

Zone 3-4: The Full Supermarket

  • Add household/cleaning section
  • Add kids/toy section
  • Install first attraction (Aquarium or Kids Play Area)
  • Health & Beauty section

Zone 5-6: The Department Store

  • Electronics section
  • Clothing & Apparel area
  • Art & prestige decorations
  • Grand Piano attraction
  • Fountain as centrepiece

Zone 7-8: The Luxury Empire

  • VIP Lounge attraction
  • Royal Fashion, Perfume Atelier
  • Crystal floors, gold inlay
  • Royal Feast Counter
  • Golden Gates entrance

🔑 Special Zone Features

VIP Zone (Zone 7)

  • Separate entrance for VIP customers (higher spending, no queue)
  • VIP Lounge unlocks here
  • All displays in VIP zone receive +20% budget from eligible customers
  • Requires 22,000+ LP to attract Celebrity customers

Farm-to-Table Section (Zone 3+)

A dedicated farm-themed section that can be placed in any zone:
- Farm Market Stand (4×3)
- Farm Corner Display (4×4)
- Herb Garden decorations
- Farm Fresh Basket display
- All items in section: +10% Fresh premium on top of normal rate

💰 Expansion Cost Multipliers

If a player wants to expand a zone early (before the unlock level), they can pay with Cash:

Before unlock level Not available

🏆 Store Rating

The overall store receives a Star Rating (0–5 stars) based on:
- % of displays stocked
- LP score tier
- Customer satisfaction (review ratings)
- Zone utilisation (tiles used vs. available)
- Number of active attractions

Rating Bonus
⭐ 1 Star Base
⭐⭐ 2 Stars +5% all customer spend
⭐⭐⭐ 3 Stars +10% spend, +1 Shopper Card/day
⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4 Stars +15% spend, +2 cards/day, Mystery Shopper 2×
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 5 Stars +25% spend, +5 cards/day, daily Cash bonus
