# 🪙 18 — Coin-Op Items

Coin-op items are automated revenue generators placed in the store that earn coins on a timer without requiring customer interaction. They also contribute Luxury Points, increasing the class of customers the store attracts.

## ⚙️ Coin-Op Mechanics

### Collection
- All coin-op items require manual collection (tap to collect) OR automatic collection via the **Cashier Upgrade** (Lv.30)
- Each item shows a progress bar filling towards maximum capacity
- At maximum capacity, the item stops earning until collected
- Visual indicator: filling progress bar, coin animation when ready

### Upgrade System
Each coin-op item can be upgraded through **3 tiers**:

| Tier | Cost | Earnings Boost | Timer Reduction |
|------|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Tier 1 | Base | — | — |
| Tier 2 | 500–2,000 coins | +25% | −10% |
| Tier 3 | 2,000–8,000 coins | +50% | −20% |
| Tier 4 | 8,000–30,000 coins | +100% | −30% |

### Placement Strategy
- Coin-op items near **Attractions** earn 10% more (increased foot traffic)
- Coffee Machine near checkout: +5% all customer budgets (queuing customers buy impulse items)
- ATM near luxury section: attracts Businessman/Socialite customers
- Arcade Cabinet near toy section: synergy with Family Shoppers
