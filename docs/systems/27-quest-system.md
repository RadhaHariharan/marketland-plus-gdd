# 📋 27 — Quest System

The quest system provides structured goals that guide progression, reward consistent play, and introduce new mechanics. Three quest types keep the experience fresh: Daily Quests, Story Quests, and Farm Quests.

---

## 📅 Daily Quests

Daily Quests reset every 24 hours. Players receive **5 daily quests** each day:
- 3 Easy (quick tasks)
- 1 Medium (moderate effort)
- 1 Hard (significant effort)

### Sample Daily Quests:
| Quest | Type | Reward |
|-------|------|--------|
| Restock 5 displays | Easy | 100 coins + 5 QD Cards |
| Serve 10 customers | Easy | 150 coins + 5 Product Cards |
| Harvest 3 crop types | Easy | 50 FT + 3 Harvest Cards |
| Earn 1,000 coins from sales | Medium | 300 coins + 10 XP |
| Complete 1 processing job | Easy | 20 FT + 2 Harvest Cards |
| Run a Flash Sale campaign | Medium | 500 coins + 15 Shopper Cards |
| Stock a Farm Mode display | Easy | 100 coins + 5 FT |
| Earn coins from 3 coin-op items | Easy | 200 coins |
| Have 3 displays active simultaneously | Medium | 400 coins + 10 XP |
| Achieve 4-star customer satisfaction | Hard | 800 coins + 20 XP + 1 Cash |

### Daily Quest Streak Bonus:
| Streak | Bonus |
|--------|-------|
| 3 days | +50% quest rewards |
| 7 days | +100% + 1 Cash |
| 14 days | +150% + 5 Harvest Cards + 1 Cash |
| 30 days | +200% + Exclusive decoration |

---

## 📖 Story Quests

Story Quests guide the player through major milestones and introduce key features. They do not reset.

### Story Quest Arc Chapters:
| Chapter | Title | Key Tasks | Reward |
|---------|-------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Opening Day | Place first display, earn first coins, hire first supplier | 500 coins + 10 XP |
| 2 | Down on the Farm | Plant first crop, harvest, send to store | 200 FT + 50 XP |
| 3 | Growing Empire | Unlock Zone 2, add 3 new displays | 1,000 coins + 100 XP |
| 4 | Fresh & Local | Enable Farm Mode on 2 displays | 300 FT + 5 Harvest Cards |
| 5 | Market Maestro | Earn 5,000 coins in a single day | 2 Cash + 200 XP |
| 6 | Animal Kingdom | Place Chicken and Cow pens | 500 FT + 100 XP |
| 7 | Processing Power | Build the Bakery Oven, make bread | 500 coins + 5 Product Cards |
| 8 | Campaign Launch | Run your first campaign | 1,000 coins + 15 Shopper Cards |
| 9 | Luxury Horizon | Reach 1,000 LP | 5 Cash + 500 XP |
| 10 | The Full Chain | Complete one full multi-step crafting chain | 10 Cash + 1,000 XP |

---

## 🌾 Farm Quests

Farm Quests are specific to the farm and reward Farm Tokens:

### Sample Farm Quests:
| Quest | Requirement | Reward |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Planting Pro | Plant 10 crop plots simultaneously | 50 FT |
| Harvest Bounty | Harvest 100 total crops | 100 FT |
| Full House | Have all animal pens occupied | 200 FT + 5 Cash |
| Processing Master | Complete 50 total processing jobs | 150 FT |
| Chain Reaction | Complete 3 multi-step crafting chains | 300 FT + 10 Harvest Cards |
| Seasonal Farmer | Harvest seasonal bonus crops 3 times | 100 FT |
| Rainbow Chase | Harvest during Rainbow weather | 50 FT + 5 Harvest Cards |
| Farm Army | Have 3 workers active simultaneously | 200 FT |
| Green Thumb Star | Earn 3 stars on any crop | 150 FT |
| Animal Whisperer | Achieve 100% happiness on 3 animal types | 250 FT + 5 Cash |

---

## 🎯 Quest UI

- Quest panel accessible from bottom navigation (📋 tab)
- Progress bars for active quests
- Completed quests show green checkmark and tap-to-claim button
- Story quest progress shown as a visual chapter map
- Farm quests on a separate sub-tab
