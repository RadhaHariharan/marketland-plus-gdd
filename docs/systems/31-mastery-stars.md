# ⭐ 31 — Mastery Stars

Mastery Stars are the long-term engagement system that rewards dedicated players for specialising in specific products, crops, and animals. There are two parallel mastery tracks: **Store Mastery** and **Farm Mastery**.

---

## 🏪 Store Mastery

Each display type earns mastery through **total customer purchases** from that display.

| Mastery Level | Purchases Required | Bonus |
|-------------|------------------|-------|
| ⭐ 1 Star | 100 purchases | +5% coins per sale |
| ⭐⭐ 2 Stars | 500 purchases | +10% coins, chance of bonus coins |
| ⭐⭐⭐ 3 Stars | 2,000 purchases | +20% coins, exclusive display skin unlocked |

### Display Mastery Benefits Stack:
A Bread Shelf at 3-star mastery earns 20% more than a fresh Bread Shelf.
Combined with Farm Mode (+40%) and a Grand Sale campaign (+50%):
**Total multiplier: 1.20 × 1.40 × 1.50 = 2.52× base value**

---

## 🌾 Farm Mastery

Farm Mastery covers crops, animals, and processing buildings (detailed in [13-farm-mastery.md](../farm/13-farm-mastery.md)).

### Farm Mastery Summary:
| Track | Metric | 3-Star Requirement |
|-------|--------|-------------------|
| Crops | Harvests | 300 per crop |
| Animals | Collections | 500 per animal |
| Buildings | Jobs completed | 500 per building |

---

## 🌟 Combined Mastery Score

The **Total Mastery Score** combines store and farm mastery:
- 1 mastery star = 1 point
- Maximum possible stars: ~200 (70+ display types + 19 crops + 9 animals + 16 buildings) × 3 stars each

### Mastery Rank Titles:
| Score | Title |
|-------|-------|
| 0–25 | Trainee Shopkeeper |
| 26–50 | Junior Manager |
| 51–100 | Store Manager |
| 101–150 | Senior Manager |
| 151–200 | Area Director |
| 201–300 | Regional Executive |
| 301–400 | Master Merchant |
| 401–500 | Grand Master |
| 500+ | Legendary Tycoon |

## 🎨 Mastery Skins

Reaching 3-star mastery on a display unlocks an exclusive **display skin** — a visual reskin that makes the display look premium/themed.

| Display | 3-Star Skin |
|---------|------------|
| Bread Shelf | Artisan Bakery Display |
| Wine Rack | Crystal Wine Cellar |
| Luxury Fashion | Gold-Trimmed Boutique |
| Royal Feast Counter | Royal Banquet Table |
| Beer Fridge | Craft Brewery Tap Wall |
| Perfume Display | Jewelled Perfume Cabinet |

---

## 📈 Mastery XP Acceleration

Higher mastery levels also speed up future leveling:
- Each 3-star display: +2% XP from all customer purchases
- Each 3-star crop: +1% XP from all harvests
- Full Farm Mastery (all 3 stars): +50 FT bonus per day
- Full Store Mastery (all 3 stars on all displays): +5% all coin earnings permanently
