# 💳 35 — Monetization

SuperStoreTycoon is built on a player-first monetization philosophy. Every design decision regarding monetization is tested against a single question: **"Does this respect the player's time and make the game better?"**

---

## 🏛️ Core Monetization Principles

| Principle | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| ⚡ No Energy System | Players are **never** forced to stop playing |
| 🚫 No Paywall | All content is reachable through regular gameplay |
| 🌿 Farm Tokens Never Sold | Farm Tokens cannot be purchased with real money — ever |
| ⏩ Cash Speeds, Never Gates | Premium currency only accelerates timers; it never locks content |
| 🎁 Fair Monetization | Every premium feature has a free equivalent (just slower) |
| 🔒 No Loot Boxes | Spending Cash always results in a known, specific outcome |

---

## 💵 Cash (Premium Currency) Spending

Cash is the only real-money currency. It is also earnable through play (+1 per level).

### Cash Sinks (What Players Spend Cash On):

| Item | Cash Cost | Free Alternative |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| Instant crop harvest | 1–3 Cash | Wait for harvest timer |
| Skip processing timer | 1–5 Cash | Wait for processing job |
| Instant supplier delivery | 2–5 Cash | Wait 30 min–4 hr |
| Repair storm-damaged shipment | 1 Cash | Accept reduced quantity |
| Premium decorations | 5–20 Cash | No free equivalent (cosmetic only) |
| Extra delivery truck slot | 10 Cash | Use existing 2 slots |
| Unlock zone immediately | 10–30 Cash | Wait for level requirement |
| Skip a quest | 3 Cash | Complete quest normally |
| Rare animal direct purchase | 15–50 Cash | Unlock through leveling |
| Exclusive event cosmetics | 5–15 Cash | None (limited cosmetics) |
| Greenhouse slot unlock | 5 Cash | Earn with Farm Tokens |

## 📺 Rewarded Ads (Optional)

All ads are **strictly optional** and always player-initiated:

| Ad Reward | Watching Time | Frequency Limit |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| +1 Quick Delivery Card | 30 seconds | 5× per day |
| +30 min shipment skip | 30 seconds | 3× per day |
| Small coin bonus (500 coins) | 30 seconds | 5× per day |
| Double next harvest yield | 30 seconds | 3× per day |

> 💡 **Design Note:** Rewarded ads are never shown automatically. Players must tap an "Ad" button to opt in. They are shown in the game HUD as an opportunity, not a requirement.

---

## 🎟️ Season Pass / Farm Pass (Monthly Subscription)

An optional monthly subscription available via in-app purchase:

### What It Includes:
| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| 🌱 Daily Seed Pack | 5 random seeds delivered daily |
| 🎴 Daily Card Pack | 3 Shopper + 3 QD + 3 Product + 3 Harvest |
| 🐄 Exclusive Animal Skins | 2 exclusive skins per season |
| ⭐ XP Boost | +20% XP on all actions |
| 🏗️ Exclusive Building Skin | 1 farm building visual reskin |
| 🎊 Exclusive Seasonal Events | 3 pass-exclusive quests per month |

### What It Does NOT Include:
- No Farm Tokens
- No exclusive gameplay content (all quests completable without pass)
- No permanent power advantage
- No exclusive story content

> 💡 **Design Note:** The Season Pass is purely about enriching the experience — more cosmetics, slightly faster progression, and exclusive aesthetics. The entire game is playable, enjoyable, and winnable without the Season Pass.

## 💰 Monetization Projections

| Player Type | Avg Monthly Spend | Model |
|------------|-----------------|-------|
| Free Player | $0 | Earns Cash through leveling; completes all content |
| Light Spender | $2–5 | Occasional Cash purchases for timer skips |
| Regular Spender | $5–10 | Season Pass subscriber |
| Heavy Spender | $10–25 | Season Pass + occasional large Cash purchases |

---

## 🛡️ Anti-Pay-to-Win Guarantees

1. **All displays reachable** without spending real money
2. **Leaderboards** based on time-played and strategy, not spending
3. **Farm Tokens** have their own economy, 100% free-to-earn
4. **Neighbours** only see each other's LP and achievements — no spending display
5. **Cash rewards** from leveling ensure even free players have premium currency
6. **No energy = no "energy wall"** that premium currency bypasses
