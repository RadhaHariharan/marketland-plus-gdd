🎉 32 — Special Events

Special Events are time-limited in-game celebrations tied to real-world seasons and festivals. They bring exclusive content, boosted rewards, new customers, and special decorations to Marketland+.

📅 Annual Event Calendar

🌸 Bloom Festival (Spring)

Duration: 14 days (March–April)
Theme: Nature's renewal, fresh starts

Event Features:

  • Cherry Blossom Trees available as limited decorations (+60 LP)
  • Lavender boost — all lavender yield ×1.75 during event
  • Flower Fortune coin-op item available (180–280 coins/25 min)
  • Spring Collection appears in Collections panel
  • Event Quest Line: 10 spring-themed quests with exclusive rewards

Exclusive Customer (Spring): 🌸 Garden Party Guest

  • Budget: 400–650 coins
  • LP Required: 5,000
  • Only buys from Fresh displays
  • Leaves a Lavender gift (5 FT) after each purchase

☀️ Beach Blast (Summer)

Duration: 14 days (June–July)
Theme: Summer holiday, tropical vibes

Event Features:

  • Tropical Decorations — beach umbrellas, palm trees, surfboards
  • Surf Simulator coin-op item (250–400 coins/35 min)
  • Tourist Customer (exclusive to Beach Blast): Budget 350–600 coins; buys refreshments, sunscreen, snacks
  • Cold beverages sell at +60% during event
  • Ice cream display (limited event display) available

Exclusive Customer: 🏖️ Tourist

  • Budget: 350–600 coins
  • LP Required: 3,000
  • Buys: Juices, smoothies, fresh produce, sunscreen
  • Leaves camera icon (rare collection item)

🍂 Harvest Festival (Autumn)

Duration: 14 days (September–October)
Theme: Bountiful harvest, community celebration

Event Features:

  • Pumpkin decorations, scarecrows, hay bales
  • Harvest Festival Booth display available (farm-themed)
  • Harvest Mega Event campaign gets +20% bonus during this period
  • All crops +10% coin on top of normal Autumn bonus = +20% total
  • Prize Wheel coin-op (300–500 coins/45 min)
  • Exclusive Harvest Collection (8 items) with 2,000 coin reward

Special Rule: The Great Harvest

For 48 hours at the event peak:
- All farm production 3× speed
- Harvest Cards cost 0 (free) for those 48 hours
- Mass harvest achievement unlockable

🎃 Halloween Haunt

Duration: 10 days (October)
Theme: Spooky mystery, treats

Event Features:

  • Jack-o-Lanterns available (30 LP each)
  • Haunted Market Stand — spooky version of Farm Market Stand
  • Mystery Shopper rate: 5× normal
  • Spooky Customer: wears costume, pays normal + leaves trick-or-treat candy (collection item)
  • All displays renamed with spooky names (cosmetic overlay)
  • 1 Cash reward for completing all 5 Halloween quests

❄️ Winter Wonderland

Duration: 21 days (December)
Theme: Holiday magic, warmth, giving

Event Features:

  • Christmas Tree (+80 LP) — most LP-efficient single decoration
  • Snow overlay on store and farm
  • Gift Dispenser coin-op (200–350 coins/30 min)
  • Gift Shopper exclusive customer: buys for others, double purchase rate
  • Hot beverages: +50% during event
  • Gift Boxes as collectibles — given by neighbour visits

Exclusive Customer: 🎁 Gift Shopper

  • Budget: 300–500 coins
  • Always buys 2 items per visit (one for themselves, one as gift)
  • Leaves a wrapped gift (random card) after each visit

💕 Valentine's Market

Duration: 7 days (February)
Theme: Romance, gifts, flowers

Event Features:

  • Heart decorations available (+25 LP)
  • Couples visit together (2 customers sharing 1 visit, double budget pooled)
  • Chocolate and perfume displays earn +50% during event
  • Limited display: Valentine's Gift Counter (farm flowers + chocolates)
