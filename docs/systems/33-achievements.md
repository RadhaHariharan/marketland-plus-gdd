# 🏅 33 — Achievements

Achievements celebrate player milestones, reward mastery, and provide long-term goals that span the entire game. They are divided into categories and award XP, Cash, coins, and Farm Tokens.

---

## 🏅 Achievement UI

- All achievements visible in the Achievement panel
- Progress bars for multi-step achievements
- Completed achievements show gold badge + claim button
- "Achievement Feed" shows recent community achievements
- Total achievement score displayed on player profile
