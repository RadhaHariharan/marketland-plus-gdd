🏪🌾 Marketland+ ⚙️ Systems

🎴 26 — Card System

Cards are the tactical layer of Marketland+. Four card types let players manipulate customer behaviour, deliveries, products, and harvests in strategic ways. Cards are earned through play and spent for powerful short-term effects.

🎴 Card Type Overview

Card Symbol Effect Earn Rate Max Hold
Shopper Card 🔵 Triples one customer's budget 2/level, attractions, neighbours 50
Quick Delivery Card 🟢 Speeds up a shipment by 1 hr 5/level, neighbours 100
Product Card 🟡 Instant stock / insurance / upgrade 5/level 30
Harvest Card 🟠 Instant harvest / double yield / skip job 3/level from Lv.10+ 30

🔵 Shopper Cards

How they work: Tap to play on a customer currently in the store. That customer's budget is tripled for their current visit.

Best Uses:

  • Play on a Tycoon or Celebrity customer for maximum effect
  • Use before a Campaign to stack bonuses
  • Use when luxury displays are fully stocked and ready

Earn Shopper Cards:

Source Amount
Level up +2 per level
Attraction visit milestone +1 per 100 attraction visits
Neighbour gift +1–3 per gift
Quest reward +5–15 per quest
Achievement reward Variable

Campaigns using Shopper Cards:

Campaign Cards Required
Flash Sale 5 Shopper Cards
Farm Fresh Festival 10 Shopper + 5 Harvest
Grand Sale 20 Shopper Cards
Harvest Mega Event 30 Shopper + 10 Harvest
VIP Night 15 Shopper Cards

🟢 Quick Delivery Cards

How they work: Apply to any in-transit shipment to reduce its remaining timer by 1 hour. Stack multiple cards on the same shipment.

Best Uses:

  • Speed up a 4-hour premium supplier delivery
  • Use when stockroom is critical and no farm goods available
  • Stack 3–4 cards to get near-instant delivery

Earn Quick Delivery Cards:

Source Amount
Level up +5 per level
Neighbour visit +1–2 per visit
Daily quest +10 per quest
Achievement Variable
Rewarded ad (optional) +1 per ad

Earn Product Cards:

🟠 Harvest Cards

How they work: Three distinct uses for the farm:

Use Effect Cost
Instant Harvest Instantly harvests all ready crops in a zone 1 Card
Double Yield Next harvest in a zone yields 2× 1 Card
Skip Processing Job Instant-completes one processing job 1 Card

Earn Harvest Cards:

Source Amount
Level up (Lv.10+) +3 per level
Farm quest reward +5–15
Rainbow weather +2–5 (bonus yield event)
Collection completion +2–5
Farm mastery milestone +5 per star achieved

🎲 Card Strategy Tips

💡 Design Note: Cards should feel powerful but not required. The game is fully playable without ever using a card — they are accelerators and satisfying power-ups, not essential mechanics.

Situation Best Card
Celebrity customer just arrived 🔵 Shopper Card → 3× budget
Shipment 3 hours away, shelves empty 🟢 Stack 3 Quick Delivery cards
Heatwave wilting premium crops 🟠 Harvest Card → instant harvest
Grand Sale campaign needs 20 Shoppers Accumulate and save them
Storm damaged incoming shipment 🟡 Product Card → Insurance
