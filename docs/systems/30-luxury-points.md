# 💎 30 — Luxury Points

Luxury Points (LP) are the store prestige score that determines which customer types visit. Unlike other currencies, LP is purely accumulated and never spent — it only grows as players invest in decorations, attractions, and premium displays.

---

## 💎 LP Accumulation Sources

| Source | LP Earned |
|--------|----------|
| Small decoration | 3–10 LP |
| Medium decoration | 10–30 LP |
| Large decoration | 30–80 LP |
| Rare/prestige decoration | 80–200 LP |
| Seasonal decoration | 30–80 LP |
| Floor upgrade (per tile) | 5–200 LP (depends on floor type) |
| Wall upgrade (per section) | 3–50 LP |
| Coin-op item placed | 5–50 LP |
| Attraction placed | 50–500 LP |
| Display tier upgrade | 5–25 LP per upgrade |
| Store zone expansion | 100–500 LP per zone |
| Achievement: store milestones | 50–500 LP per achievement |

## 📈 LP Growth Strategy

### Early Game (Lv.1–20): 0–300 LP
- Focus on basic plants and floor tiles
- Every decoration purchase at this stage significantly impacts LP
- Priority: Tile floor + Ficus plants + Welcome Sign

### Mid Game (Lv.20–50): 300–5,000 LP
- Add attractions (Aquarium, Kids Play Area)
- Upgrade floors to Wood or Marble
- Place Art & Prestige decorations
- Priority: First major attraction + Chandelier + Fountain

### Late Game (Lv.50–80): 5,000–22,000 LP
- Crystal floors in key zones
- Royal art pieces, large sculptures
- VIP Lounge attraction
- Gold Sconces and Crystal Chandeliers
- Priority: VIP zone decorations + Grand Piano

### Prestige (Lv.80+): 22,000–30,000+ LP
- Gold Inlay floors throughout
- Royal Portrait, Abstract Installation
- Golden Gates entrance
- All luxury attractions upgraded to Tier 3

## 🔮 LP Aura System

Certain high-LP decorations and attractions project an **LP Aura**:
- All other decorations within the aura radius gain a 10–25% LP boost
- Auras do not stack infinitely (diminishing returns after 3 overlapping auras)
- The Fountain has the largest aura radius (8 tiles)

### LP Aura Sources:
| Item | Aura Radius | LP Boost |
|------|------------|---------|
| Fountain | 8 tiles | +20% |
| Grand Piano | 5 tiles | +15% |
| VIP Lounge | 7 tiles | +25% |
| Crystal Chandelier | 4 tiles | +15% |
| Living Wall | 3 tiles | +10% |

---

## 📊 LP Display & UI

- LP is displayed as a **permanent counter** in the HUD (💎 icon)
- LP tier badge displayed on store sign and player profile
- LP progress bar shows distance to next customer unlock
- "LP Record" screen shows all-time LP milestones
